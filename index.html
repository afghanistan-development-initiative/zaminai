📸 Upload photo → instant detection · 94ms inference
Disease detection
YOLO identifies wheat rust, leaf blight, powdery mildew and 50+ crop diseases from a phone photo — with confidence score and treatment in the farmer's language.
Pest identification
Aphids, locusts, stem borers — detected and counted per photo. AI recommends pesticide type, dose, and timing for your exact field size.
Yield estimation
Photo of crop head → AI estimates maturity stage and optimal harvest window, cross-checked with satellite NDVI data.
Soil health
Photo of soil → identifies soil type, moisture, compaction issues. Combined with SoilGrids API data for a complete soil profile.
